Pawan Kalyan

Advancements and Achievements

Advancements

Winning the National Award and six Nandi Awards for the film "Tholi Prema".

Being named one of India's Top 5 Heroes in a Star India survey in 2014.

Being ranked 24th in the Forbes India list of top 100 Celebrities in 2018.

Receiving a Filmfare Award, a SIIMA Award, a CineMAA Award, and a Santosham Film Award.

Founding the Jana Sena Party.

Making a mark as a director and screenwriter, with his films Johnny and Gudumba Shankar receiving critical acclaim.
